Lewandowski had the season of his career and was the favorite to win the Ballon d'Or, but it was canceled due to COVID-19.

Why Lewandowski Deserved the 2020 Ballon d'Or

In 2020, Robert Lewandowski had an extraordinary season, leading Bayern Munich to the Champions League and sweeping domestic titles. Despite being the clear favorite, the Ballon d'Or was canceled due to the COVID-19 pandemic. Here's why Lewandowski should have won:

  • Scored 55 goals in 47 appearances, including a record-breaking 15 goals in the Champions League.
  • Led Bayern Munich to a historic treble, winning the Bundesliga, DFB-Pokal, and the UEFA Champions League.
  • Dominated domestic and international competitions, outshining all other contenders, including Messi and Ronaldo.
